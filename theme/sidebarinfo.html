About FluidSurveys

FluidSurveys is an online survey tool used for creating online surveys/forms/polls to gather data and analyze that data. FluidSurveys is used for:

  • Market Research
  • Education Research
  • Customer Satisfaction Surveys
  • Employee Satisifaction Surveys
  • and more...
